Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Arrow Puzzle ("we", "us", "the app") respects your privacy. This document explains what data the app collects, why, and your rights regarding it.

1. Data we collect

We collect only the minimum necessary data to provide and improve the gameplay experience:

Category Source Why we collect it
Account Info (Email, Name, Profile Avatar) Firebase Auth / Google Sign-In To manage player accounts, keep level progress safe, and display profiles.
Payment Details (Purchase history) RevenueCat To verify subscription/premium status and unlock in-app purchases.
Push Notifications (Push token) Firebase Cloud Messaging To send game updates, daily reminders, and streak notifications.
App Activity (Game events, level completion) Firebase Analytics To analyze gameplay patterns and improve game design.
Diagnostics (Crash logs, performance metrics) Firebase Crashlytics To detect crashes and optimize stability across devices.
Advertising IDs (Ad interactions, device identifiers) Google AdMob To show personalized or non-personalized ads (removable via Premium).

2. How we use your data

  • App Functionality: Delivering gameplay progress, processing visual skins, providing logic hints, and managing daily challenge streaks.
  • Account Management: Authenticating user sign-in and maintaining profile details across devices.
  • Analytics: Understanding general user patterns and layout engagement to refine updates.
  • Crash Tracking: Spotting and fixing software errors dynamically.
  • Advertising: Displaying supported game promotions.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. The third-party services listed below process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase Services (Auth, Analytics, Crashlytics, FCM): Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat Inc.: Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Your rights

You can:

  • Delete your account: Request account deletion directly in the app via Settings → Account → Delete account to immediately remove your authentication profile.
  • Request Data Export: Contact us at kajalkarkar007@gmail.com to receive a copy of your stored gameplay attributes.

Data is fully purged from our active services within 30 days of an account deletion request.

5. Security

We enforce secure communication using HTTPS protocols for all data transfers. Local game credentials and secret variables are stored securely on the device using AES-GCM encryption in Android Keystore (SecureDataStore).

6. Children

Arrow Puzzle does not target or knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal details, they will be deleted immediately.

7. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: kajalkarkar007@gmail.com.
